About VisionCare

Dedicated to excellence in eye care since 2001. Our mission is to preserve and restore vision with compassion, expertise, and cutting-edge technology.

Our Story

VisionCare Eye Clinic was founded in 2001 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell with a simple mission: to provide exceptional eye care that puts patients first. What started as a small practice has grown into one of the region most trusted eye care centers.

Today, our team of 15 specialists serves over 20,000 patients annually, offering comprehensive services from routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures. We remain committed to our founding principles: personalized care, continuous innovation, and unwavering dedication to patient outcomes.

Our state-of-the-art facility features the latest diagnostic and surgical technology, allowing us to deliver precise treatments with minimal recovery times. We invest in ongoing training and education to ensure our team stays at the forefront of ophthalmic advances.
