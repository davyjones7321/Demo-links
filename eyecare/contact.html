Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about appointments and our services.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, a list of current medications, and any previous eye records if available.

How long is a typical appointment?

A comprehensive eye exam usually takes 45-60 minutes. Surgical consultations may take longer depending on the complexity.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please call our office to verify your specific coverage.

Can I bring someone with me?

Yes, you're welcome to bring a friend or family member. For pediatric appointments, a parent or guardian must be present.
