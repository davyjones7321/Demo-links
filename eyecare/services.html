Our Services

Comprehensive eye care services tailored to your needs. From routine exams to advanced surgical procedures, we're here to help you achieve optimal vision.

Cataract Surgery

Cataracts are a common age-related condition where the eye's natural lens becomes clouded, affecting vision quality. Our advanced laser-assisted cataract surgery removes the clouded lens and replaces it with a premium artificial intraocular lens (IOL).

We offer multiple IOL options including monofocal, multifocal, and toric lenses to correct distance vision and reduce dependence on glasses.

  • Blade-free laser-assisted technique
  • Premium lens options
  • Quick recovery time
  • Outpatient procedure

LASIK

LASIK (Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis) is a life-changing procedure that can free you from glasses and contact lenses. Using precision laser technology, we reshape the cornea to correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism.

Our clinic uses state-of-the-art wavefront-guided technology for personalized treatment plans that optimize visual outcomes.

  • Blade-free, all-laser procedure
  • Customized wavefront treatment
  • Rapid visual recovery
  • High patient satisfaction

Retina Care

Our retina specialists provide comprehensive diagnostic and treatment services for all retinal conditions. Early detection and prompt treatment are crucial for preserving vision in cases of retinal disease.

We treat conditions including age-related macular degeneration (AMD), diabetic retinopathy, retinal vein occlusions, and epiretinal membranes.

  • Advanced retinal imaging
  • Intravitreal injections
  • Laser treatments
  • Vitreoretinal surgery

Pediatric Eye Care

Children's eyes require specialized care to ensure proper visual development. Our pediatric optometrist creates a friendly, comfortable environment for young patients while providing thorough examinations and treatments.

We offer comprehensive pediatric services including vision screenings, glasses prescriptions, amblyopia (lazy eye) treatment, and myopia management.

  • Child-friendly environment
  • Vision therapy
  • Myopia control
  • School screenings
Schedule Consultation

Glaucoma Treatment

Glaucoma is a group of eye diseases that damage the optic nerve, often associated with elevated eye pressure. Early detection through regular eye exams is essential, as vision loss from glaucoma is irreversible.

We offer comprehensive glaucoma management including medication therapy, laser treatments, and surgical interventions tailored to each patient's needs.

  • Advanced pressure testing
  • OCT nerve imaging
  • Laser trabeculoplasty
  • Minimally invasive surgery
