About Us

Three decades of excellence in luxury real estate

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by real estate visionary Robert Prestige, our firm began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional service to those seeking the finest properties in the most desirable locations.

What started as a boutique agency in Prosperville has grown into one of the region's most respected luxury real estate firms, with a portfolio that spans from coastal estates to mountain retreats, urban penthouses to countryside manors.

Today, under the leadership of Robert's daughter, Victoria Prestige-Chen, the firm continues its tradition of excellence while embracing innovation and contemporary design sensibilities.
