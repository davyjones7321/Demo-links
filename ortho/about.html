Our Story

Leading orthopedic care since 1998

Premier Orthopedic Clinic was founded in 1998 with a singular mission: to provide world-class orthopedic care that helps patients return to active, pain-free lives. What started as a small practice has grown into one of the region's most trusted orthopedic centers.

Our team of board-certified orthopedic surgeons brings over 100 years of combined experience to treating everything from simple fractures to complex spinal surgeries. We believe in a patient-first approach, taking the time to understand each individual's needs and goals.

We invest continuously in the latest surgical techniques and technology, including robotic-assisted surgery and minimally invasive procedures that mean faster recovery times and better outcomes for our patients.
