About FlexRestore

Helping people move better and live better since 2015

Our Story

FlexRestore Physiotherapy was founded in 2015 by Dr. Kavitha Rao, a sports physiotherapist who saw the need for patient-centered, evidence-based rehabilitation in her community. Her vision was simple: treat the person, not just the pain.

Today, our team of 8 licensed physiotherapists serves over 5,000 patients annually with specialized programs for sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, chronic pain management, and neurological rehabilitation.

We invest in continuous education and modern equipment to ensure our patients receive world-class treatment in a warm, supportive environment.
