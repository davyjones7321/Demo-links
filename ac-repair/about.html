About ArcticAir

Keeping homes and offices cool since 2012

Our Story

ArcticAir AC Services was founded in 2012 by Rajesh Kumar, a certified HVAC engineer with a vision to provide honest, affordable, and professional air conditioning services. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of 25+ trained technicians serving over 8,000 customers annually.

We specialize in residential and commercial AC solutions, handling everything from new installations and routine maintenance to emergency repairs. Our commitment to quality workmanship and transparent pricing has earned us the trust of families and businesses across the region.
