Our Story

Serving Fixit City Since 2010

SpinFix Appliance Repairs was founded with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable washing machine repair services at fair prices. What started as a one-technician operation has grown into Fixit City's most trusted appliance repair company.

Over the past 15+ years, we've completed over 5,000 repairs and built lasting relationships with thousands of families in our community. We believe in treating every customer like family—providing the same quality of service we'd want for our own homes.
