About CareFirst

Your trusted healthcare partner since 2008

Our Story

CareFirst General Clinic was founded in 2008 by Dr. Priya Sharma with a mission to provide accessible, compassionate healthcare to families in Healthville and surrounding communities.

What began as a small practice has grown into a comprehensive medical center with multiple specialists and state-of-the-art diagnostic facilities. We believe that quality healthcare should be within everyone's reach.

Our team of dedicated physicians and support staff work together to ensure every patient receives the care and attention they deserve.
