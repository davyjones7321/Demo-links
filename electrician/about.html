Our Story

Powering Progress Since 2009

VoltPro Electrical Services was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, safe, and affordable electrical services to the Current City community. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted electrical contractors.

Over the past 15 years, we've completed over 2,500 projects ranging from simple outlet replacements to full-scale commercial installations. Our commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction has earned us an outstanding reputation.

We believe that excellent electrical work shouldn't come with a premium price tag. That's why we offer competitive rates without compromising on materials or craftsmanship. Every project we undertake receives the same attention to detail, whether it's a emergency repair at midnight or a planned renovation.
