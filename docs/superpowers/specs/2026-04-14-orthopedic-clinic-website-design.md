# Orthopedic Clinic Website Design Spec

**Date:** 2026-04-14

## Overview
Professional static orthopedic clinic website with 6 pages: Home, About, Services, Gallery, Testimonials, Contact. Corporate style with navy/white color scheme.

## Color Palette
- Primary: `#1a365d` (navy)
- Primary Dark: `#0f2442`
- Primary Light: `#2c5282`
- Accent: `#e53e3e` (red for CTAs/highlights)
- Light Background: `#f7fafc`
- Text Dark: `#1a202c`
- Text Light: `#718096`
- White: `#ffffff`

## Typography
- Headings: `Playfair Display`, serif
- Body: `Open Sans`, sans-serif

## Pages

### 1. index.html (Home)
- Navbar (shared)
- Hero: full-screen background with navy overlay, clinic name, tagline, CTA button
- Services overview: 5 cards with icons
- Why Choose Us section
- CTA section
- Footer (shared)

### 2. about.html
- Navbar (shared)
- Hero with navy background
- About section with clinic history
- Team section: 3 orthopedic surgeon cards with photo, name, specialty
- Mission statement
- Footer (shared)

### 3. services.html
- Navbar (shared)
- Hero
- Services grid: 5 service cards
  - Joint Replacement
  - Spine Surgery
  - Sports Injury
  - Fracture Care
  - Arthritis Treatment
- Each card: icon, title, description, price range
- Footer (shared)

### 4. gallery.html
- Navbar (shared)
- Hero
- Photo grid with hover effects
- Footer (shared)

### 5. testimonials.html
- Navbar (shared)
- Hero
- 6 patient testimonial cards with star ratings
- Footer (shared)

### 6. contact.html
- Navbar (shared)
- Hero
- Contact form: name, email, phone, message, submit
- Google Maps embed
- Contact info: address, phone, email, hours
- Footer (shared)

## Shared Components

### Navbar
- Logo/Clinic name (left)
- Navigation links (right)
- Sticky on scroll
- Mobile: hamburger menu

### Footer
- 3 columns: About, Quick Links, Contact info
- Copyright

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1023px
- Desktop: 1024px+

## Interactions
- Smooth scroll
- Hover effects
- Form validation
- Mobile menu toggle
