Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by course. For NEET/JEE programs, students must have completed their 10th or 12th grade with minimum qualifying marks. For other courses, there are no strict prerequisites. Contact us for specific requirements.

Do you offer scholarship programs?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for students demonstrating exceptional academic performance. Scholarships can cover up to 50% of tuition fees. Scholarship tests are conducted quarterly.

Can I attend a trial class before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to attend a free trial class. Schedule a visit to experience our teaching methodology and interact with our faculty members.

What study materials are provided?

All enrolled students receive comprehensive study materials including textbooks, practice papers, previous year question banks, and access to our online learning portal with video lectures.
