Cockroach Control

Eliminate cockroaches and prevent reinfestations

Cockroaches are more than just a nuisance—they can spread diseases and trigger allergies. Our comprehensive cockroach control service uses advanced baiting and insecticidal treatments to eliminate existing infestations and create barriers to prevent future problems.

Our Process:

  • ✓ Thorough inspection to identify species and nesting areas
  • ✓ Targeted bait placement in cracks, crevices, and harborage points
  • ✓ Insecticidal spray treatment along baseboards and entry points
  • ✓ Sealing of entry points and potential harborage sites
  • ✓ Follow-up treatment and monitoring

Termite Treatment

Protect your property from costly termite damage

Termites cause billions of dollars in property damage each year, and they can be active in your home for years before you notice signs. Our comprehensive termite treatments include both liquid soil treatments and baiting systems to eliminate existing colonies and prevent new infestations.

Our Process:

  • ✓ Complete inspection for mud tubes, wood damage, and swarmers
  • ✓ Termidor or equivalent liquid treatment around foundation
  • ✓ Sentricon bait station installation for ongoing protection
  • ✓ Wood repair recommendations for existing damage
  • ✓ Annual monitoring and maintenance plans
Starting at $399

Bed Bug Removal

Complete eradication with heat and chemical treatments

Bed bugs have made a dramatic comeback in recent years, and they're notoriously difficult to eliminate without professional help. Our integrated approach combines heat treatments that penetrate everywhere bed bugs hide with targeted chemical applications for complete eradication.

Our Process:

  • ✓ Thorough inspection of beds, furniture, and harborage areas
  • ✓ Canine inspection for accurate detection
  • ✓ Heat treatment to 135°F throughout affected areas
  • ✓ Chemical treatment in cracks, seams, and crevices
  • ✓ Encasement recommendations and follow-up visits
Starting at $299

  • ✓ Elimination of standing water sources

Rodent Control

Humane removal and exclusion to keep them out

Mice and rats can cause extensive property damage, contaminate food supplies, and pose serious health risks. Our rodent control program focuses on humane trapping, exclusion work to seal entry points, and sanitation recommendations to make your property less attractive to rodents.

Our Process:

  • ✓ Inspection to identify entry points and rodent species
  • ✓ Tamper-resistant bait stations and traps placement
  • ✓ Exclusion work to seal gaps and entry points
  • ✓ Decontamination and cleanup of nesting areas
  • ✓ Monthly monitoring and maintenance programs
