Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How much does pest control cost?

Pricing varies based on the type of pest, severity of infestation, and property size. We offer free inspections and quotes with no obligation. Most general pest treatments start around $129.

Are your treatments safe for pets and children?

Yes! We prioritize the safety of your family and pets. We use EPA-registered products and offer eco-friendly, low-toxicity options whenever possible.

How long does treatment take?

Most residential treatments take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on property size and the type of service. Heat treatments for bed bugs may take 6-8 hours.

Do I need to leave my home during treatment?

For most treatments, you can remain in your home. We'll let you know specific instructions before your appointment. For heat treatments, brief relocation may be recommended.

Do you offer guarantees?

Yes! We stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. Ask about our service warranties when you call.

How often should I schedule pest control?

We recommend quarterly service for ongoing prevention. Some situations may require more frequent visits. We'll create a customized plan for your needs.
