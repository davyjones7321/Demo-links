About SafeGuard

Over 18 years of protecting homes and businesses in Securetown

Our Story

SafeGuard Pest Solutions was founded in 2005 by John Mitchell, a third-generation pest control professional with a vision to provide safer, more effective pest management solutions.

What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Securetown's most trusted pest control companies, serving thousands of residential and commercial clients across the region.

Our commitment to using environmentally responsible products and methods has remained at the core of everything we do, ensuring that we protect not just your property, but also the health of your family and the planet.
